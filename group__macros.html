Uses 1u (unsigned) so that BIT(15) == 0x8000 holds under cc65816's 16-bit int (since chantier A1, 2026-05-08). With signed 1, 1 << 15 overflows to -32768 on a 16-bit int and compares unequal to the (long-promoted) 0x8000 literal.
